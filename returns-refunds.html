Returns & Refunds

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

At The Sharp Shoppe, we stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship with our Razor Sharp Guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your sharpening service, please review our policy below.

1. Razor Sharp Guarantee

If your tools are not sharpened to your satisfaction, you must contact us within 14 days of receiving your returned items. We will provide a prepaid shipping label for you to send the item back to us, and we will re-sharpen it at no additional cost.

2. Refund Policy

If we are unable to sharpen your tool to an acceptable standard after a re-sharpening attempt, or if a tool is deemed un-sharpenable upon initial inspection, we will issue a full refund for the sharpening cost of that specific item.

3. Shipping Costs

Initial shipping costs to send your tools to us are non-refundable unless a promotional prepaid label was provided. Return shipping costs for re-sharpening under our guarantee are covered by The Sharp Shoppe.

4. Damaged in Transit

If your tools are damaged or lost during return transit from our facility to your address, please contact us immediately. All return shipments are insured, and we will initiate a claim with the carrier to compensate you for the value of the lost or damaged tools.
