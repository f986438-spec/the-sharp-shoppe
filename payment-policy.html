Payment Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

This Payment Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding billing, payments, and pricing for services provided by The Sharp Shoppe.

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. All payments are processed securely through our Shopify-hosted checkout system.

2. Payment Timing

Payment is required in full at the time of placing your order online, before you ship your tools to us. If additional services are required or requested after we receive your tools, we will send an invoice for the additional amount which must be paid before the tools are returned.

3. Pricing and Taxes

All prices listed on our website are in US Dollars (USD). Prices are subject to change without notice. Applicable state and local sales taxes will be calculated and added to your total at checkout based on your shipping address.

4. Custom Pricing

For exotic blades, specialty tools, or bulk commercial orders, custom pricing may apply. In these cases, we will provide a quote via email. Work will only commence once the quote is approved and payment is received.
